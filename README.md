# Dhaka 8 No Ward: Election Madness 🗳️🥚🍅🎤🍌

A fast-paced, satirical arcade action game capturing the chaos, humor, and drama of local elections in Dhaka. Play as notorious candidates—Mirza Abbas or Nasir Uddin Patwary—and duke it out with eggs, tomatoes, sandals, mics, bananas, and biting one-liners. Outsmart and out-throw your opponent to win the ultimate "izzat" battle!

## Game Overview

**Election Madness** turns the legendary political rivalry of Ward 8 into a comedic battleground. You select your candidate and engage in real-time duels, flinging various objects at your opponent in a dynamic arena. Keep your "izzat meter" high by dodging enemy attacks, accurately hurling projectiles, and unleashing bangla election-day taunts for that extra edge.

## Key Features

- **Real-Time Throwing Battles:** Click/tap to launch eggs, tomatoes, sandals, microphones, bananas, and more at your opponent.
- **Dynamic Dialogues:** Each candidate delivers randomized taunts, slogans, and iconic quips in mid-battle—delivered in Bengali and styled for comic effect.
- **Candidate Selection:** Choose between candidates, each with unique voice lines and "attitude."
- **Izzat/Life Meter:** Keep your respect (izzat) intact—get hit too many times, and it's all over.
- **Live Match Stats:** Track total matches, wins per candidate, and high scores.
- **Comedic Animations:** Silly UI, vibrant art, wobbly text, and rich sound effects keep every game session fresh and hilarious.
- **Bonus Rounds:** Earn extra points with special items (Biryani packets and more).

## Gameplay

1. **Launch and Select Candidate:** Choose between Mirza Abbas and Nasir Uddin Patwary.
2. **Start the Battle:** Use your mouse or finger (mobile) to aim and throw items; each item has a unique sound and effect.
3. **Dodge & Counter:** Move quickly to avoid attacks from your rival. Target them for counter-throws.
4. **Generate Dialogues:** Enjoy a parade of taunts, slogans, and banter as projectiles fly!
5. **Win by Izzat or Points:** Either reduce your opponent's life/izzat meter to zero or dominate with the highest score.

## Controls

- **Desktop:** Click to aim and throw.
- **Mobile:** Tap on-screen to flick items.
- **Live Stats:** See running totals of matches and per-candidate victories on the dashboard.

## Assets

- **Graphics:** Sprite images in `assets/images/`
- **Audio:** Place your sound files (`catch.mp3`, `fail.mp3`, `bg_music.mp3`, custom effects) in `assets/audio/`

## Deployment

- 100% Vanilla JS & CSS—zero dependencies.
- Push to GitHub and deploy instantly on Vercel or your favorite static site host.
